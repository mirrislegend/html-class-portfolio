The Best Movies According to Aaron

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Matrix

The breakout scifi movie of the new millenium.

The Nightmare Before Christmas

Anti-hero lead, stop-motion animation, and a plot full of subversion of expectations. What's not to love?

Monty Python and the Holy Grail

The lords of absurdist humor are at it again. Truly this is tied with Life of Brian. Both are masterpieces.
